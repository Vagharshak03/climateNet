PROFESSIONALIZATION
One of the differentiating elements of UFAR is professionalization. A factual proof lies in the fact that more than 92% of the alumni of the 15 UFAR promotions are working. To achieve a very high employability of its students, the university constantly studies the needs of the market. A vector of professionalization is therefore the design of courses and programs in relation to the expectations of companies. Similarly, teaching alternates theory and practice, with many case studies and industrial projects, led by a multitude of professional teachers in the field. To strengthen this axis of professionalization, Accelerator 28 (hyperlink to the accelerator) was created at the end of 2020, a support structure promoting entrepreneurship in Armenia. Professionalization internships in Armenia and abroad allow students to be best prepared for integration into the world of work. It should also be considered that many students work while pursuing their studies, which considerably strengthens the study/profession link. Finally, dedicated teams are in charge of supporting students in the emergence of their professional projects, while helping them acquire the necessary job search techniques. Specific support on the subject of integration is provided in the form of compulsory courses and personalized advice.
INTEGRITY
The value of integrity was at the very foundation of the creation of UFAR. The university considers it necessary to develop and ensure a culture of integrity. As soon as they enter the University, students know that impartial evaluation, examination monitoring and disciplinary committees make UFAR an institution of integrity. This integrity is all the more important as students appropriate it and become its bearers in their daily actions and in their professional lives. Indeed, integrity is implemented in UFAR procedures but also in concrete projects with a strong ethical connotation. The university's desire is indeed to embody this value of integrity through concrete actions. This is how UFAR has set up a Plan for the Reintegration of Student Soldiers (PRES) and a program to help students with academic difficulties upon returning from military service, the PREMâ€™S (Plan for the Reintegration of Ex-Military Students towards Success). This makes it possible to show examples of integrity to students to encourage them to continue to embody this value as ambassadors of UFAR.
GATEWAY TO THE WORLD
A differentiating element of UFAR compared to its competitors is the link that UFAR creates for its students with Europe and the world. By obtaining a double degree (European/French and Armenian), by deepening foreign language (particularly French), by being in frequent contact with foreign teachers, UFAR students benefit from particularly strong international links. The solidity of the relations between UFAR and its associated universities reinforces UFAR as a true gateway to Europe. Finally, UFAR takes particular care to develop the French language among its students and in the country. The differentiating element of UFAR "Gateway to the World" has different components, the main one being the development of language learning. An investment in language learning will continue to be made not only for students but also for staff. Connecting students with many teachers (80 per year) strengthens access to international realities. International enrichment also comes from UFAR's links with different structures in French-speaking countries (OIF, AUF, local authorities, French-speaking agencies, etc.).
