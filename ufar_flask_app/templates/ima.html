To be a student at the University of Toulouse 3 Paul Sabatier,studying at UFAR

  • The program aims to:

    - Enable students to achieve a high level of proficiency in the various areas of computer science concerned with AI (specific treatments for digital imaging, 3D graphics, and audio-visual content; representation, processing and access to structured data and text-based information, data
    management and their transformation into usable knowledge; integration of AI in robotic systems, by going deeper into the vision and interaction aspects with speech and natural language etc.).
    - Develop expertise based on the issues raised by the areas of application of AI, in order to provide appropriate solutions.

    Diplomas

    This academic program is carried out in partnership with the University of
    Toulouse III - Paul Sabatier. Two diplomas are awarded at the end of the Master’s cycle: a French diploma from the University of Toulouse III – Paul Sabatier, Master in Computer science with Specialization in Artificial Intelligence: Foundations and Applications (IAFA), and an Armenian diploma from the French University in Armenia.

  • Program overview 

    The Master in AI is a full-time face-to-face course spread over 4 semesters, corresponding to 120 credits (ECTS).

    Teaching language: English.

    An internship of at least 5 months is compulsory at the end of M2, in a laboratory or in a company. In M1, several scenarios are possible:

    -2 months Internship,

    -Tutored project at the university,

    -Group project in S8, on topics proposed by academics or industrial partners,

    -Small group research work in S8,

    -"Masterpiece" group project, in S9 and S10, requiring the implementation of skills acquired in AI.
